## Quick Start

To begin:

1. Log into your guilhermecxe account.
2. Click on **“New Doc Space”** on the dashboard.
3. Enter a name for your space (e.g., "Project Alpha").
4. Click **Create**.

Your documentation space is now ready! You can add pages, organize content, and invite collaborators anytime.

## Tip

Use folders and tags to keep your docs organized as your project grows.
