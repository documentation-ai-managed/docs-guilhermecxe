---
title: Cannot Access Documentation Space
description: Resolve login and permission errors in guilhermecxe.
---

## Symptoms

- Seeing "Access Denied" or "Not Found" errors
- Unable to see your doc spaces

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login">
    Log out and sign back into your guilhermecxe account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    Make sure you have permission to view the doc space, or ask the owner to invite you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Try reloading after clearing cache if issues persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
