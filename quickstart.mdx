## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have:
- A valid email address for sign-up
- Existing accounts on Google Ads, Meta Ads Manager, or TikTok Ads
- No credit card required for the 14-day free trial
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Access Dashboard

Follow these steps to create your BigLink account and reach the dashboard in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit BigLink" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [biglink.app](https://biglink.app) and click **INICIAR TESTE GR√ÅTIS**.

    No credit card needed for the 14-day trial.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Enter your email and create a password. BigLink sends a verification email.

    Check your inbox (including spam) and click the verification link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Dashboard" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Log in to see your personalized dashboard at `https://biglink.app/dashboard`.

    Your AI marketing agent is ready to analyze data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your First Data Sources

BigLink unifies data from multiple platforms. Connect one or more ad accounts to start.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Google Ads" icon="google">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize Google" icon="key">
        In the dashboard, select **Connect Google Ads** and sign in with your Google account.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Accounts" icon="database">
        Choose your ad accounts and grant read-only permissions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="success">
      Data syncs in seconds. Metrics like CPA and ROAS appear automatically.
    </Callout>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Meta Ads" icon="facebook">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Connect Meta" icon="key">
        Click **Connect Meta** and log in to Ads Manager.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Grant Access" icon="shield">
        Select ad accounts and confirm permissions for campaign data.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    Data from Meta campaigns loads instantly for AI analysis.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="TikTok Ads" icon="music">
    Follow similar steps: Click **Connect TikTok**, authorize, and select accounts.

    TikTok creative and performance data integrates seamlessly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Make Your First AI Query

Once connected, ask the AI about your marketing data. Use natural language questions.

````jsx
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="CPA Query">
    Type: `Qual foi meu CPA hoje?`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="ROAS Analysis">
    Type: `Onde devo escalar verba no Google Ads?`
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Campaign Comparison">
    Type: `Compare ROAS entre Meta e Google esta semana.`
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
````

<Callout kind="tip">
The AI analyzes unified data from all sources and responds with actionable insights in seconds, including charts and recommendations.
</Callout>
